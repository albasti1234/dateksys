About the Hospital

Al-Hayat Hospital

Over a quarter century of healthcare excellence

Our Story

Al-Hayat Hospital was founded in 2001 in the heart of Amman to be a distinguished medical facility offering the highest standards of healthcare. We started with two departments and ten doctors, and today we proudly operate eight specialized departments with over 50 doctors serving more than 100,000 patients annually.

Our Vision

To be the leading hospital in Jordan and the region in providing comprehensive healthcare with international standards, placing the patient at the core of our focus.

Our Mission

To provide integrated, high-quality medical care through distinguished professionals and modern technologies, while upholding humanitarian values and professional ethics.

Our Facilities

6 fully equipped operating rooms
20 ICU beds
150 inpatient beds
24-hour central laboratory
Advanced imaging unit (CT + MRI)
Comprehensive hospital pharmacy
Multiple outpatient clinics
Spacious parking facilities

Accreditations

Accredited by the Higher Health Council and the Jordanian Private Hospitals Association, with commitment to ISO 9001 international quality standards.

Leadership

Al-Hayat Hospital is led by an administrative and medical team with over 30 years of experience in hospital management and healthcare in Jordan and the region.
