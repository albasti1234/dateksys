Our Story

Over three decades of healthcare excellence

Royal Medical Center was founded in 1995 in the heart of Amman to be a distinguished medical facility offering the highest standards of healthcare. We started with a limited number of departments, and today we proudly operate 45 specialized departments with over 250 doctors serving hundreds of thousands of patients annually.

Our Vision

To be the leading medical center in Jordan and the region in providing comprehensive healthcare with international standards, placing the patient at the core of our focus.

Our Mission

To provide integrated, high-quality medical care through distinguished professionals and modern technologies, while upholding humanitarian values and professional ethics.
